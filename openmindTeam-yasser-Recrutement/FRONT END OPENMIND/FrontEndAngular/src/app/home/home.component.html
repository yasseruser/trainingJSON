FAQs

Find answers to common questions about the application process and platform usage.

How do I apply ?

To apply for a position, simply navigate to the 'Apply' page and follow the instructions provided.

What documents do I need ?

The required documents may vary depending on the position Generally, you will need a resume, cover letter and any relevant certifications or qualifications.

How long is the application process ?

The length of the application process can vary. It typically takes around 2-4 weeks from the submission of your application to receiving e response.

Can I apply for multiple positions ?

Yes, you can apply for multiple positions. However, please ensure that you meet the qualifications and requirements for each position before applying.

How can I check my application status ?

To check the status of your application, log in to your account and navigate to the 'Applications' section. Here, you will find updates on the progress of your application.
